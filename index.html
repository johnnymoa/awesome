#About

A collection of projects built to entertain you.

Soothing Snake

  • Based on the classic snake game.
  • Play using arroy keys or swiping motion.
  • You can't loose! (if you bite the tail the snake becomes shorter).
  • Mobile compatible.
  • Slither away!
  • Play it here.

Draw Items

  • Draw shapes using your mouse, erase by right clicking.
  • Use arrow keys to change rotation and size.
  • Upload custom backgrounds and download your work.
  • Try it here.

The Programmer's Guide To The Galaxy

  • A classic space shooter.
  • On Desktop: Arrow keys , P for pause, M for music.
  • On Mobile: Swipe to move.
  • Have fun crossing the galaxy!
  • Play it here.

Pixelotron

  • Draw with the mouse.
  • Erase by right clicking.
  • Use arrow keys to shift your drawing.
  • Try looping it.
  • Try it here.

Flash Capital

  • Flash cards to learn all the capitals of the world!
  • Switch card: arrows or swipe left/right.
  • Flip card: arrows up/down or tap.
  • Mobile compatible.
  • Expand your brain.
  • Start learning here.

Flappy Cube

  • A low budget homemade Flappy Bird clone.
  • Tap screen or press space to Flap.
  • Mobile compatible.
  • Flap away!
  • Play it here.

Road Rage

  • You accidentally drove to the wrong side of the highway, how long will you survive?
  • Left/Right arrows or swipe to change lanes.
  • Mobile compatible.
  • Play it here.
